I feel like I was deceived with this beer as nowhere on the can does it say it was a sour. I only found out when I googled and discovered it was a tweak from a previous sour brew. It tasted like fizzy orange and lemon squash. Managed to finish the whole drink (maybe egged on by the novelty of drinking out a new glass) but I definitely won't be buying this again.
