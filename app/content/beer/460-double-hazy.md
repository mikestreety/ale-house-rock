I've finally completed the triple. Finished the Hazy series. The double hopped version of @brewdogofficial's Hazy Jane (and the little brother of Triple Hazy), this beer has the best of both worlds. The kick that comes with a DIPA without the overbearing of alcohol found in the triple. This beer got better the more I drank it, and the 7.4% was a curve ball that snuck up on me. Would highly recommend trying the original first then moving onto this. Killer beer.
