Not sure if this strictly belongs on an Ale review account, although this has now turned into a "beer in a vessel" kind of place. Rescued this from the desolate office the other week as it was due to go out of date soon. This lager was nice and I would happily drink again. Preferably in a garden or other outdoors place with it being chilled. Nice hoppy taste, easy sippin' and went down well.
