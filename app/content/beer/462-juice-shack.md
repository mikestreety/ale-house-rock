I've been thinking about this "Tropical Milkshake IPA" all day, trying to work out what I thought about it. It certainly reminded me of a milkshake, and had a juicy taste to it but did I enjoy it? I'm still not sure. I drank the whole can, I didn't turn my nose up at all but I didn't crave for more once I'd finished. A bit like the "Cream Soda Pilsner" I had the other week, it seems like a novelty. I'm glad I experienced it, but I wouldn't do it again anytime soon.
