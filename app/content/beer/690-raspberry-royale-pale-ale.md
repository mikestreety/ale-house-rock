Now this was an experience. A raspberry "pudding" ale from a mainstream supermarket. It didn't really taste like beer at all and is definitely one you would want after dinner. It was light, bordering on a sour-style, but it was sweet. I quite enjoyed it, I think, but it wouldn't be at the top of my list to rush out and buy again. My wife enjoyed it (and went back for a second swig when trying) and she doesn't even like beer. I really struggled to score this one!
