I'd never had barley wine before, so I was intrigued as to what it would be like. If I had to sum it up in one word, I think I would choose "rich". If normal beer was a normal glass of squash, this tasted like drinking the stuff neat. It was thick with a good flavour and you wouldn't want more than a sip each time - which is good, as it's 10%. I would have it again, but I can't say I would buy it over a beer given the choice.
