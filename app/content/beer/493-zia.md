What a tough beer to rate. A 9.5% Tiramisu Pastry stout which was as sickly as it sounds. 440ml is too much of this beer - even 330ml is too big. First few sips of this were promising; you could smell the coffee without getting too close. The flavours were sweet and the kind of thing I was after yesterday evening after a meal out. I opened this at 8pm, and by 10:30pm I had only made it two thirds of the way through the glass - with some still left in the can. Very heavy, very thick and very sweet. Picked up at Tesco
