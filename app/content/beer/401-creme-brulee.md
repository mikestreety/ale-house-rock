One of the great things about running a beer review Instagram is that people buy you new beers. I prefer a stout after dinner, and this was a perfect dessert. Had a sweet aftertaste which wasn't too sickly nor was it too heavy. The drinkability of this beer was very moreish and, although I don't often buy stouts, I will keep an eye out for this beauty. Good work @darkstarbrewco
