Brewdog and Northern Monk - can't go wrong, eh? Opened this with anticipation but when I took the first sip of this, I was convinced it wasn't that good. However,  as the drink went on, it got better and better to the point where I didn't want it to end. Smooth drinking and great taste. Remnants of a DIPA without being as strong. Picked this one up in Tesco.
