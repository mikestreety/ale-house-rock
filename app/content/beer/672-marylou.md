I wouldn't normally pick a pale ale, as they tend to be light, flavourless and a bit boring, however, this "Pales" box from @thefuss.club has forced me to try some proper ones. This pale from @verdantbrew is absolutely fantastic. I had to stop myself from drinking it all in one go. It tasted like it should have been more than the 5% that it is. It had a morish light-but-fruity flavour. Definitely one of the best pales I've had.
