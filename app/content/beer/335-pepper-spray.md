An interesting concept from @mikkellerbeer and @peoplelikeusdk. An IPA brewed with black pepper. First few sips were nothing special but once I got into the groove, and took a breather, the black pepper started to really come alive. Not overwhelming, but enough for you to notice in the aftertaste. Was spot on as an accompaniment to a pepperoni pizza. 👌 It's a tough one to score as I often rate beers based on taste and if I would buy it again. I'm glad I've had this, it was a nice taste but I can't say I would reach for it if I saw it on a shelf (except maybe for someone else)
