I think, based on pound per ml, this is the most expensive.beer I've ever bought. It was also the mod challenging to open as it had a cork and made me realise I don't have a bottle opener 😆 As for the actual beer, Saisons have a particular hard-to-describe taste anyway (is it hoppy?). This one was particularly fruity (as expected) and was enjoyable. It was a great beer to share with a friend, sipping it slowly and catching up. This would go great with a picnic or similar. It's certainly a "special occasion" beer.
