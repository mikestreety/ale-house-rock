Beer delivery day means beer on a Tuesday. This one tastes exactly how you imagine a beer festival to taste. Slightly cloudy (putting that down to not having time to settle) but an enjoyable pint nonetheless. Taking a sip of this transports you to days of yonder, where you would choose an ale from a stained leaflet purely based on the name, stagger up to beer belly tubbed man and ask for some in your glass. Not quite suited to home drinking, but a good nostalgia trip. @riverside_brewery
