Second beer from @riverside_brewery and one that is a lot clearer, and comes with a good solid taste. I think it is taking my taste-buds a bit of time to adjust from the smooth hipster craft beers to the rugged ales. Ordering from Riverside is certainly a comforting taste excursion; taking me back to "old man" pubs with friends, sitting by open fires in the winter. Lovely flavours and easy to drink. A bit of a bitter tang after each sip.
