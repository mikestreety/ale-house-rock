Instead of chocolate this year, Ma and Pa bought me beer for Easter. I always feel extra disappointment when I'm not a fan of beer someone has bought me, but this one was a bit flat with its flavour. It was a light IPA that ticked all the boxes except the taste. Went down easy enough while chatting with friends on zoom but highly forgettable.
