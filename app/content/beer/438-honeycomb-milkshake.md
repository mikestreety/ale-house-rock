How the hell did they make this beer taste like a milkshake?! The first few sips threw me right into a 'shake flashback. Once my taste-buds had settled, this wonderfully easy-drinking beer ticked a lot of my boxes. Despite being great tasting, I wouldn't add it to my regular "buy again" list. It's a one-off beer, a birthday treat. Certainly not one for everyday. A great beer though 🐝
