First beer out of the @flavourlyhq box from this week is a @fourpure classic. A "tangerine pale", I was hoping for a Juicebox style beer but, instead, was faced with a pale ale with an orangey aftertaste. Palettable, and I'll happily drink the other 4 but certainly not one of my favourite Fourpure beers.
