This was a lovely change of pace. I could imagine ordering pint after pint after pint of this Session IPA from @deyabrewery while sitting in a pub (preferably a garden of sorts). Refreshing, light and goddam easy to drink - I actually made an audible disappointed noise when I picked up my glass and found it empty. It wasn't overly packed with flavour - which is perfect for a session, but that is why it misses out on a point.
