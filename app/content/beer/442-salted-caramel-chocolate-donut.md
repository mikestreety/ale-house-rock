This @unbarredbrewery beer grew on me. The first few sips came after the mead, so my mouth wasn't quite sure what to do with all that information, but as the drink went on it got better and better. Mrs Ale House provided me with a brownie and caramel ice cream halfway through this can which paired nicely - it's not often you find a beer that works well with dessert. A bit too much of a "salt" taste (I know it's salted caramel) for my liking but still pleasurable.
