When we were in Suffolk, I asked the gentleman working in @hopstersipswich what his favourite beer was. This was it, a "pudding IPA". It was an odd taste to begin with (and, unsurprisingly didn't go with my dinner) but once I got into it I started to really enjoy it. Wouldn't rush for it again, but was certainly as experience I'm glad I had. Had a bit of yeast in the can, which I wasn't expecting
