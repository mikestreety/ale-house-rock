Unusually, this beer got less enjoyable as I drank it. The taste of alcohol was too prominent for me. I've had other 8% beers which are barely noticeable but can imagine some people enjoying the sting. Felt like a strong DIPA for strong DIPA sake, rather than making a good beer. This one started at a 6 or 7, but ended much lower.
