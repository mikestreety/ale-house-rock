Had this been 6 months ago, I wouldn't have been seen dead with a craft beer in my fridge. Lockdown, however, seems to have altered my perspective on such beer. This collaboration between @brewdog and @cloudwaterbrew is an absolute corker. Unexpected sediment at the end meant I couldn't quite get the last mouthful I was expecting, but good nonetheless. Perfect accompaniment to Tony Hawk's 1 & 2 on PS4
