This was an unusual one to get used to and not really one to have with dinner (more of a post-dinner beer, really). A consistency that reminded me of my pineapple juice and lemonade days (with no fizz) combined with a slight after-taste of the mallow. Very sweet and the first beer (in over 450) that both my wife and I liked. Can't say I would buy this again, but enjoyed it nonetheless.
