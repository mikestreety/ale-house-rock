As per Ale House lockdown rules, when a beer delivery turns up, one must be drunk that evening. As it's only a Monday, I picked this 2.5% Radler (shandy) from the lot. It's exactly as you would expect from looking at the can: a slightly alcoholic lemonade & does not taste at all like a beer (so much so my beer-hating wife said "it was nice") so I'm tentative about its appearance on this feed. Nice thirst quenching Monday night drink
