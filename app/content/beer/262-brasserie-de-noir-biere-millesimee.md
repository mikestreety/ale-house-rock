The bottle of this one was 100% the reason I picked it up. An Aldi special, the cork drew me in and the sound that accompanied it would not disappoint. Nice beer, unusual to get a 750ml bottle but was a nice novelty. Perfect for the situation or fancying a bit more but not wanting another full pint. Easy enough to drink & reasonably priced too
