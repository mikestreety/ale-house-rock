Followed up the last beer with this absolute beast of a near-pint from @fourpure. Brewed in partnership with @mothership.beer, this double IPA is an absolute stunner. Wouldn't want too many for these, but was perfect as an evening wind-down and I'm glad I've got a second one in the fridge. It was full of hops with a nice deep flavour, Fourpure have done it again.
