This DIPA was incredible. When I saw on Instagram that @deyabrewery and @thebeakbrewery were collaborating I was excited. I didn't even realise it was in shops until I walked into @palate.bottleshop today and saw the last can. It was a perfect DIPA - fruity and smooth but with a hint of bitterness to remind you you're drinking a beer & not a fruit juice. Despite going for the bigger cans from Deya, I still didn't want this 8% marvel to end.
