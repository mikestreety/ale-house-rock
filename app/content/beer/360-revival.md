This beer from @drinkmoorbeer was in my latest @flavourlyhq box. There were 4 of them in the box and after 3, I'm still not sure what to make of it. It tastes like you've just eaten a sweet and had a swig of beer (sometimes), watery (sometimes) or like a fine craft beer (sometimes). I would drink again, but re-buyability is low.
