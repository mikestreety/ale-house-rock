A surprisingly enjoyable porter from this brewery. I wasn't holding out much hope, but this was light bodied yet still had the coffee undertones you'd expect from this style. A perfect Introductory beer. I wouldn't buy it again, but would recommend to someone who only drinks light beers and wants to broaden their horizons
