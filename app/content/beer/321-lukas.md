After my last Thornbridge experience (Tart, 2/10) I was hesitant about this one. After a day in the garden, this helles style lager was perfect. Nice taste for a lager and a lovely looking can. Once the hard work of the day wore off, this turned out to be a better lager than most, but lacking anything significant. Would grab a couple of these for a BBQ but nothing more
