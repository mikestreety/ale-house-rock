Yet another installment in the "Hazy Jane" saga. I wasn't holding out much hope for it, really but it turned out nicer than I expected. I can't say I noticed much difference in the taste, maybe a bit more fruitier but definitely a gimmick. I gave the original Hazy Jane an 8, but in hindsight (and having drunk more), it deserved a 7, which is what I'm awarding this one.
