After the last few @unbarredbrewery beers I was excited about this DIPA offering. Unfortunately, it didn't live up to my expectations. I know this is an odd comment for an 8% beer, but you could taste too much of the alcohol. Strong and unforgiving - I think I'll leave this one on the shelf next time.
