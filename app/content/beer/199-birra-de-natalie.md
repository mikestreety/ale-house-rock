My 200th beer and what a beauty it was. Bought from Genoa for my parents (they were kind enough to share it with me) this sneaks up on you. The first step is sharp - the 8% cutting through, but the more sips you take, the smoother and tastier it gets; it’s fruity too. Lovely beer
