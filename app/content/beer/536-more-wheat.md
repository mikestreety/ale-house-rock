The other side of the two IPAs brewed by @verdantbrew and @thebeakbrewery. Thought it would only be fair to taste them back to back. This one had the same smoothness and full flavour as the More Oats beer did, but I much preferred the taste of this one. I can't quite explain what it was or why. Interesting experiment to brew the two beers, but seems I am a wheat man after all - I'm glad I got to taste the two next to each other.
