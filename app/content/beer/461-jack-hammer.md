Another day, another Brewdog. This beer is described on the can as a "ruthless IPA" and, while I wouldn't go so far as to call it ruthless, it was certainly more abrasive than the other IPAs from @brewdogofficial. A welcome change from the sea of pale ales in the Lock Down Survival Kit, this 7.2% beer was one that got better the more you drank it. I can almost say I _really_ enjoyed it by the time I got to the last sip.
