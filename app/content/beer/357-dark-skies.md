This is an odd one and not a usual stout from @drygate (via @flavourlyhq). There was no initial shiver you get from a full-on flavoured stout and it wasn't the usual heavy taste. Somewhere there was a hint of coffee, but nothing overpowering. A good gentle sipper, which could be mixed in with a session, rather than being a nightcap
