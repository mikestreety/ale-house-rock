Beer one of the zoom tasting evening with @laurzstreet. We both ordered the January mystery mix box from @beermerchants. Started with this one which has the most dubious description (to be served with Apple and Vinaigrette pudding?). This instantly took me back to a summer evening on Brighton beach. I wouldn't order it again, but not as bad as I was expecting. Very Belgian in taste, but not a premium flavour.
