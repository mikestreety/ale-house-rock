This. Was. Sickly. A super thick, sweet stout which took me a long while to drink. Slow sips over a good hour or more and was a very filling beverage. Definitely a drink to end the evening and you wouldn't want any food with it. I'm not sure I would have it again. Picked up from the @unbarredbrewery tap room I visited last week..
