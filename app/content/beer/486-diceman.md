How can a brewery which gets IPAs and, in fact, every other type of beer so right get a stout so wrong. I could just about taste the chocolate coffee notes that are so often found in a stout below the slightly watery flavour. Didn't really enjoy this beer at all, which is a shame really. I did manage to finish it though.
