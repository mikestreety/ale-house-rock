This is a classic Aldi beer in that it pours well, smells great and tastes like an anticlimax. I cracked this open and could smell the chocolate and orange straight away - as a big Terry's fan, I was looking forward to this. The taste, however, was artificial and not very orange (or chocolate)-like.
