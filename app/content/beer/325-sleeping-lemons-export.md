My Wild Beer delivery turned up yesterday and thought I would give them a crack. Bought this as it was one of @wildbeerco's most popular beers (well, this is the stronger Export version at 6%), but I struggled to see why. I believe this taste and cider-like balance is bordering on the "sour" trend which is popular at the moment, as it was rather lemony in flavour. Definitely a summer "garden party" beer - maybe one to be enjoyed while wearing a trilby and deck shoes. Not for me I'm afraid
