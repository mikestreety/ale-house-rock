This beer was as disappointing as I was expecting. I don't tend to buy "sour" beers but this came in the Lockdown kit from Brewdog, so I had to try it. First taste is watery, followed by the sour hit. After swallowing, you get a hint of a "cool" breath - much like an Airwaves chewing gum. It got better as it went on but I still couldn't finish it. This beer did, however, confirm that my non-drinking wife does find sour beers "ok".
