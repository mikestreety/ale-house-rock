As a Brewdog investor (for better or worse) I got sent a case of 24 of these and this was the last one. I'd been telling myself for weeks I'd review it but never got round to it. Such a nice, easy IPA. At 6% it packed a little fruity punch, but I would easily pick this over most of the other Brewdog offerings. If you see one, pick it up and give it a taste.
