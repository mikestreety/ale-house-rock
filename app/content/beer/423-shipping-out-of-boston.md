A lager that's this colour?! And a lager that tastes good?! Wonderfully light and lager-like with a decent flavour to boot. Went very well with the takeaway curry I had this evening, but don't think it I would have enjoyed it as much if I was drinking it solo. I do, however, wish all lagers tasted like this. Despite the good review, it is still a lager and still has lager qualities, so can't get max marks from me. If this was a lager review account instead though, then well, I'd have bigger problems to worry about (like why I'm running a lager review account 😉).
