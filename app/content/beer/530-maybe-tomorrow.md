I've heard plenty of good things about @goodthingsbrewingco, so when I saw they'd made my favourite type of beer, with one of my favourite breweries (@northernmonk), sold at one of my favourite shops (@palate.bottleshop) I had to pick one of these up - it did not disappoint. Plenty of depth of flavour with this one - you can taste the 8%. It's certainly a special beer - not one for a drinking session or a regular tipple, but went down very nicely last night while playing a game.
