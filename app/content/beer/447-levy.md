Had this sitting in my fridge for a while as I was reluctant to drink it. I've had it a few times before and always been unimpressed. It's trying to be a black IPA (or a "light" stout) but falls short of the mark. There are some coffee/chocolate notes there but they get overwhelmed by the watery taste.
