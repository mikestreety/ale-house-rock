After a months hiatus from posting new beers (don't worry, I was still drinking beer - I just fancied a few weeks of drinking some old favourites without having to think of scores), I thought I would get back on the wagon and post this beauty. I've had a few @buxtonbrewery beers in the past and some have been a bit meh, but this one is incredible. Smooth, very drinkable and a drinking 8%. Reminded me of a couple of Fourpure beers. Excellent stuff Buxton!
