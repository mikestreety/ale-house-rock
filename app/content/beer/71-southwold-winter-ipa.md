Brewed by Adnams for Marks & Spencer. Tasting it I can see why they don’t sell it under the “Adnams” name. It’s certainly not up to scratch and doesn’t compare to their other beers (was drinking a Ghost ship just yesterday). Very floral with an odd aftertaste. Wouldn’t buy it again.
