Bought this one from Aldi a couple of days ago. Super smooth drinking and, for the price is a very good NEIPA. It's never going to be as good as one of the small brewery New England IPAs, but you can't ignore that it costs a third of the price. Will happily drink this again (and will pick a few more up next time I'm in store) There is certainly a "mass produced" aftertaste to it, but it's not enough to put me off. The best 10 4 Brewing (Aldi's own) beer I've had
