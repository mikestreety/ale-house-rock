Home. A @northernmonk Beer in a Northern Monk class. Had to keep reminding myself not to chug this 6.5% IPA, but it was tricky as it was so damn tasty. Full bodied and smooth, was an absolute treat for a Thursday night. I'm sure this will be sneaking into the online shopping basket again. The Monks have smashed another one out the park.
