Well, this one caught me by surprise. The catalyst for the Ald IPA I reviewed a few weeks ago, I was not expecting great things from this, based on the fact I am neither a fan of Pink IPA nor their IPA love-child. However, this was darn tasty. Crisp, flavoursome and a cut above the others. This is want Punk should taste like and I will definitely be hunting more of these down
