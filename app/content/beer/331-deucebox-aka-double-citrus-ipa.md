If there is a silver lining to this lockdown, it's my discovery of  @fourpure and their excellent delivery service. Once again, they've produced an absolutely cracking beer. Not quite as good as this one's little brother, Juicebox, but still an excellent drink. An 8.3% double IPA, this drink was a slow sipper last night but was a great way to end the week.
