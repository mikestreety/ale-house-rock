2 months ago Brewdog ran a campaign whereby they promised everyone in the UK could get free beer. A 4 pack of their Lost Lager finally rocked up yesterday, so I proper battle tested it this evening with 2 cans. Not really an evening sipping beer, but certainly did the job. One of the few lagers I would happily opt for over some of the other beers I've reviewed. Light and crisp but with plenty of flavour.
