The triple hopped version of the Hazy Jane I had a couple of weeks ago. This triple IPA packs an absolute punch at 9.5%. Certainly not one to be chugged but after a monotonous Friday, it was a great end. Felt lightheaded after a third but had a great taste with no chemical after-burn which you might expect from a beer of this ABV.  It's a great tasting beer but don't think I would queue them up very often - maybe once in a while as a treat. There is a Double Hazy, which might be a perfect combo.
