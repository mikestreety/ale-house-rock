Another DIPA from @thebeakbrewery and another absolute banger. A bit more of a hoppy/harsh DIPA, not as smooth and hazy as the other ones I've had from them. The 8.5% snacks you in the face  with this one and leaves no prisoners. A perfect one-can-of-an-evening, kind of drink. Picked up at @palate.bottleshop - looks like I'll have to go back there soon too, to pick up more Beak beer!
