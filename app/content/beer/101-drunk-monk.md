This “home brew” from the Saltdean Brewery was exchanged for some bike brake pads, which seems like a good deal. This is their stout and it is glorious. It’s got all the right flavours and smells without being too heavy. It’s chocolatey and coffee-y and tasty. Definitely a slow burner but would happily have this again and again
