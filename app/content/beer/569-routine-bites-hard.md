This is the kind of beer dreams are made of. Smooth, fruity and so very easy to drink. You forget that this tasty TIPA is packing a 10% punch - something you only get hit with when you try and do something. This beer from @deyabrewery scores a near-perfect 10. Everything ticks the box except session-ability. As easy as it would be to sit and drink this all night, I'm not sure any of my internal organs could handle it.
