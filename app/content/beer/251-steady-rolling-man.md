Going through a bit of a hipster beer thing at the moment and this is no exception. Pouring it out, I thought @laurzstreet had picked up a cider for me instead as it is very apple-juicey in colour. I wasn't holding out much hope but I was very much proven wrong. An absolutely lovely beer, easy to drink and very moreish. This is definitely one of the best hipster pale ales I've had
