Well this was an experience I would rather forget. A peanut butter milk stout (apparently the best in the shop). Was excited about this as I love peanut butter & milk and I have a fondness of stouts. It was not good, but took me the whole glass (yes, I did finish it) to find out. It's a flavour I can't put into words. I'm glad I tried it (I think?), It hasn't put me off trying other peanut butter milk stouts (I think?) but I definitely won't have this one again (I know that for sure)
