A sickly sweet, thick stout which, except the first sip, I can't say I enjoyed that much. The first taste was a novelty and gave me false hope for things to come. I finished it, but mainly out of respect for my wife who bought it for me. Talking of which - she was so repulsed by the smell on my breath, she left the room.
