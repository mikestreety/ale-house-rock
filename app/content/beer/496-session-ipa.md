I don't normally go for "Session IPAs" as can be a bit bland. This ale from @toastale, however, was full of flavour and delightfully morish. It was a shame to see the end of it. Toast Ales are made from surplus bread and all their profits go to charity - so not only did this beer taste great, I felt morally better by drinking it. A little present from my mum
