Stewart Brewing was established in Edinburgh as a local, independent micro brewery by Steve and Jo Stewart. The comapany is based on two very simple principles:-  To produce the highest quality beers possible and  to provide an exceptional service to our customers. We are dedicated to producing hand crafted, premium Scottish Beer and since its founding in 2004 it has gone from strength to strength with Stewart Brewing quickly establishing itself as one of Scotland's most successful and loved breweries.
