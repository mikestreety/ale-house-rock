On Rotation Brewery & Kitchen, located just outside Love Field in Dallas, Texas, specializes in hand-crafted, small batch beers, served across our 40 taps alongside our ever-changing lineup of eclectically curated guest taps.    You'll find our tap list stacked with exceptional craft beers, ciders, mead, and seltzers while our scratch kitchen offers a full menu of elevated brewpub staples with gluten-free fried chicken, wings, and chicken tenders as the stars of the show.    The gluten-free menu is complemented by a wide selection of gluten-free beers & beverages, including our imaginative seltzer cocktail program. And On Rotation offers a large selection of non-alcoholic beverages as well, including non-alcoholic beer.    Don't miss the award-winning Jalapeño Saison, known for its spicy nose and dry finish or the roasty Café Au Lait.
