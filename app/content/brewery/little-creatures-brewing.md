It’s those little touches that make up  Little Creatures.    From how we chose our name – honouring  the living “little creatures” (or, as most  people would call it, yeast) in our beer…  To our method of brewing the beer, using  hops specially imported from the US…    To doing little things that make a  difference at the brewery, such as taking  people on beer tours, having little chats  with customers, or creating play areas for  the little ones.    Then there’s the little things we do to try  and make a positive impact on the world  around us.    Add up all these little things and you can  really feel – and taste – the difference.
