We are a microbrewery based in Nottingham. We first starting brewing at the Flowerpot in Derby in 2011 before relocating to Basford, Nottinghamshire in 2014 in the old Shipstone’s brewery premises. Black Iris is fast becoming legendary in Nottingham but our beers can be found as far afield as Yorkshire, Cheshire, Birmingham and more. We’ve got “consistently strong artwork” from Kev Grey and have been known to put on rowdy parties with our ‘Piss Up In A Brewery’ nights. We brew beers with passion, quality ingredients and a punk rock aesthetic.
