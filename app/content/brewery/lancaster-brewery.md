Since its re-launch in November 2007 Lancaster Brewery has gone from strength to strength becoming one of the fastest growing breweries in the United Kingdom.    Brewing at full capacity with demand outstripping supply, the Brewery moved to much larger premises on Lancaster Leisure Park.    Our purpose built ‘state of the art’ Brewhouse has enabled us to brew enough of our top quality ales to supply all demands, and our Brewhouse & Tap will allow customers to take a tour and learn about how we brew our beer or simply enjoy the atmosphere.     Our stunning Brewhouse & Tap showcases a wide array of functions throughout the year with big festivals and creating a unique atmosphere for all party occasions.     We brew beer to ancient standards. We mill our own barley malt, use whole hops, our water is drawn from 80 metres below ground via our artesian well and our yeast has a heritage dating back over 200 years. It's proper beer made without taking any short cuts.
