The Three Legs Brewing Co. is an independently owned and operated Brewery and Taproom, established in 2014. We've grown from our humble beginnings in a shipping container to our new brewery space in Bexhill-on-Sea.     We brew cask, keg and barrel-aged projects from sessionable pales, seasonal sours, IPAs and classic beer styles less celebrated.    Heist Market on Norman Road in St. Leonards-on-Sea is home to our small but perfectly formed bar. You’ll find a range of our beers as well as guest beers from our favourite breweries around the country. We also have selection of sharing bottles including lambics, barrel-aged sours and saisons as well as local cider and soft drinks. We’re open 12-10 Mon-Thurs, 12-12 Fri-Sat  and 12-6 Sun.
