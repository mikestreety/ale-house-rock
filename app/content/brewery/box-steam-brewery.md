Box Steam Brewery was a family-owned brewery based in Holt, Wiltshire, founded in 2004, relaunched in 2019 and then ceased production in mid 2022.    They launched a new identity for 2019, for their growing range of cask, keg and bottled ales – all of which took inspiration from the engineering triumphs of Isambard Kingdom Brunel, the great Victorian railway engineer.    They were “committed to producing distinctive, contemporary ales using time-honoured methods - and will never compromise on quality and taste. By using steam infusion, we ensure a high standard of naturally flavoursome results, which continue to attract industry acclaim year on year.”
