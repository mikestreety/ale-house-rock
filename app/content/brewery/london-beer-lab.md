We opened our Brixton doors in mid 2013 with the aim of teaching Londoners how to make beer. We expanded operations to offer up to 30 draught lines featuring our commercially available beers, exclusive nano brews and guest beers plus a large stock of cans and bottles. We remain one of the few venues in London to stock home brewers with hops, malt and yeast.    Our commercial brewing operation is in Loughborough Junction with our nearby taproom on Nursery Road, Brixton. We are an open source microbrewery and award-winning SIBA member. We also offer home brew and brew-your-own courses.    Our taproom is a beer lovers paradise. 24 line taproom with bottles and cans from some of our favourite indie breweries, plus our own award-winning beers.
