True character isn’t made overnight. In fact, it’s something we’ve had brewing for over 245 years.    Home to Badger and Outland Beers, the Badger Brewery is a state-of-the-art, independent and family-owned brewery which now stands proudly alongside our old brewery on the banks of the River Stour in Blandford, Dorset.
