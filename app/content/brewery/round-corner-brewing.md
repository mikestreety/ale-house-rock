We believe craft beer is all about the relentless pursuit of perfection – and it’s this pursuit that drives us to make Uncommonly Good Beer.    Highly drinkable and exceptionally moreish, our beers are inspired by the character of our thriving agricultural home, the raw flavour of the East Midlands, and our international brewing experience.    We seek to brew the best example of every beer style we approach. Our high standards are paying off – the awards and accolades keep coming, year on year.
