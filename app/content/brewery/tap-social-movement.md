Based in Oxford, Tap Social Movement is an independent brewery, bakery, social enterprise, and hospitality organisation that provides training and employment for people in prison and prison leavers. To date we have created more than 100,000 hours of paid, meaningful employment for leavers.    We brew a range of modern, accessible beer highlighted by our multi award-winning core range. Visit us in Oxfordshire at our flagship Botley taproom, The Market Tap (at The Covered Market Oxford), Proof Social Bakehouse, and Lock29 (Banbury).
