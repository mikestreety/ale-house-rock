Brains is Wales’ most famous drink. It is the toast of a nation.    Still owned by the descendants of the founders that took over a fledgling brewery in a small stone building behind a Cardiff pub in 1882.     Brewed at The Cardiff Brewery, Brains' award-winning beers are now widely available across Wales and can increasingly be found in supermarkets and pubs throughout the rest of Britain.
