THE BRZN ARMS IS CLOSED. FOR NOW.     BRZN started early 2018, when we decided to stop talking about how we’d start a brewery and actually go ahead and make some great beer. We’ve been working on recipes ever since - the idea was to make beer that not only pushed boundaries but matched the quality and consistency of our favourite breweries that inspired us to kick this all off.    In early 2020 we built a brewery in a VERY SMALL YET SURPRISINGLY FUNCTIONAL shipping container in central Brighton, slinging fresh litres to takeaway through multiple lockdowns.     We opened our bar, constructed from dreams and tenacity, pinting from August 2021 - October 2024.
