The Lord Nelson Brewery Hotel is Sydney's oldest continually licensed hotel, located in the historic Rocks precinct and home of Australia's oldest pub brewery. The Lord is recognised around the world for its award winning beers, food and accommodation, which allows our guests to experience the amenities of a modern establishment with the grandeur and colonial appeal of one of the worlds most unique hotels.
