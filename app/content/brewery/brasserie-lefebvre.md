Resourcing  your celebrations  since 6 generations      Based in Belgium, the origin of the  Lefebvre Brewery goes back to 1876. The brewery offers you a wide range  of unfiltered special beers, such as Blanche de Bruxelles, Hopus, Barbãr,  the Floreffe range, ... Six generations young, the Lefebvre Brewery remains above all a family  business. Unceasingly in expansion,  they mainly work with overseas  countries by exporting around  80% of their production.    Member of the Belgian Family Brewers (www.belgianfamilybrewers.be)
