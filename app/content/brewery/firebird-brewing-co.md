A craft micro-brewery producing craft beer from around the world right here in rural West Sussex. With an ever-widening range of beers, Firebird brews something for everyone!     Alongside our commitment to brewing excellence and sustainability, we take immense pride in being an integral part of our local community, fostering relationships with independent local businesses and loyal customers.    Our fun brewery bar overlooks our working brewery and is open 7 days a week with late hours from Thursday to Saturday. Check out our socials or website for all the latest information on our weekend food trucks, live music, special events and brewery tours.
