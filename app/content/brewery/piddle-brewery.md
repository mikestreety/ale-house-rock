Crafted beside the River Piddle in deepest Dorset, for people who take beer seriously (but not life)    Since 2014, Piddle hasbeen under new ownership & management. Having completely re-evaluating the range & retiring many beers, Piddle now boasts 6 regular & 8 seasonal cask beers.   Piddle also boasts it's own Bottling Plant & has recently launched it's own Gluten Free IPA, Keg Lager & a new KeyKeg range with an AIPA - 'Wavemaker'.
