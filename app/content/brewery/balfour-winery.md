England's only range of beers and ciders created by winemakers.    The Balfour winemakers draw on Kent’s heritage for growing the finest hops, reviving forgotten varieties and combining them with modern hybrids to create beers with a clearly defined personality. Complex yet balanced, each beer is an individual, authentic expression of terroir, ingredients and the art of brewing.     All our ciders are made with 100% juice from Kent apples grown in our orchards: Russet, Cox and Bramley. Each variety is fermented separately, then blended by our winemakers to form our signature wine-like character: clean, crisp and bursting with flavour.
